The Talking Little Bee

One sunny morning,a little boy was looking at the flowers in the garden. Suddenly,a little bee came and sat on his nose.
He was afraid that the bee might sting him. He stood very still.
Then the bee gave him a wink. The little boy was shocked.

"Excuse me ,little boy.I thought you were a flower",said a little bee and flew away.The little boy was very surprised.

MORAL: Think carefully and react wisely when you experience something new.
